Treasure Hunt

Players searching for treasure hidden in someplace.
A treasure hunt is one of many different types of games with five or more players who try to find hidden objects or places by following a series of clues.Treasure hunt games may be an indoor or outdoor activity. Outdoors it can be played in a garden,indoors it can be played in a specific room, which would make the work of hiding the clues harder.Time limit is 2 hours.Spot Registations are allowed
